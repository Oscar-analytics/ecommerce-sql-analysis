# 📊 E-commerce Sales & Customer Insights Analysis

# Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed an e-commerce dataset to uncover key business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, and product performance.

Using SQL, I performed data aggregation, ranking, and trend analysis to answer real-world business questions.

---

##  Key Business Questions Answered

* What is the total revenue generated?
* Which product categories generate the most revenue?
* Who are the top customers by spending?
* Which customers spend above average?
* Who are the top customers in each region?
* What percentage of customers contribute to 80% of revenue? (Pareto Analysis)
* How does revenue change over time?

---

## Tools Used

* SQL (Joins, CTEs, Window Functions, Aggregations)
* Relational Database (MySQL)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Revenue is concentrated in a few product categories
* A small percentage of customers contributes the majority of revenue
* High-value customers exist across different regions
* Revenue trends fluctuate over time, indicating possible seasonality
